## EMAIL TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Subject Line Best Practices:
‚úÖ Include specific numbers: "80% Funded" or "$100K in Grants"  
‚úÖ Create urgency: "Deadline Approaching" or "Limited Spots"  
‚úÖ Be specific: "Your Property Qualifies" vs "Business Opportunity"  
‚ùå Avoid spam words: "Free Money", "Act Now", "Limited Time Only"

### Body Content Tips:
‚úÖ Keep it short (under 200 words for cold outreach)  
‚úÖ Lead with benefit, not your service  
‚úÖ Use bullet points for easy scanning  
‚úÖ Include ONE clear call-to-action  
‚úÖ Add social proof (once you have it)  
‚úÖ Mention "licensed and insured" for credibility

### Follow-Up Cadence:
- Day 1: Initial email
- Day 4: Follow-up #1 (different angle)
- Day 10: Follow-up #2 (add urgency/deadline)
- Day 20: Final follow-up (breakup email)

### Tracking:
- Use email tracking (HubSpot, Yesware, or Gmail extensions)
- Note which subject lines get opened
- Track which CTAs get clicks
- A/B test different approaches

---

**Pro Tip:** Personalize the first sentence of every email. Mention something specific about their property, business, or location. This dramatically increases response rates.
