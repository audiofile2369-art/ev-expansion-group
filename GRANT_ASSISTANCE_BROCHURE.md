## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: How long does the grant process take?**  
A: Typically 30-60 days for approval, then 30 days for installation. Total timeline: 60-90 days.

**Q: What if my grant application is denied?**  
A: We have a high approval rate due to experience. If denied, we help you reapply or find alternative funding sources. With Option C pricing, you only pay if successful.

**Q: Do I have to provide public access to the chargers?**  
A: For NEVI-funded projects, yes. Chargers must be publicly accessible 24/7. You can set pricing to generate revenue.

**Q: Can I charge for usage?**  
A: Yes! You set the pricing. Many property owners generate revenue that covers the 20% match and ongoing costs.

**Q: What about maintenance?**  
A: SK Signet chargers come with warranty. We offer maintenance packages, or you can handle it yourself. Average maintenance is minimal (similar to ATM machines).

**Q: How much electrical capacity do I need?**  
A: We conduct a free site assessment. Most commercial properties have sufficient capacity or can be upgraded. Electrical upgrades are eligible for grant funding.

**Q: What happens if my property sells?**  
A: NEVI grants typically require 5 years of operation. We help structure agreements to transfer obligations or can assist with alternative solutions.

**Q: Can I add more chargers later?**  
A: Absolutely! Once you have one installation, expanding is easier and faster.
